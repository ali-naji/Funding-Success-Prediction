Funding Success Classifier

Our Algorithm is a classification model that takes in information about your prospected project like its description, estimated number of backers, financial goal, and whether you will enable or disable communication with backers to a classifier that is trained on thousands of project funding successes and failures with high prediction accuracy to predict the full-funding success chances of your project. Note that success here means fully-meeting financial goals within deadline of project. Projects partially funded by assigned deadline are failures
